Get a Quote

Fill out the form below and we'll get back to you!

How will you be sending your cards? *

If you choose local drop-off, we'll provide the address after we review your submission.

Upload photos of your cards *

Please upload clear photos of the front and back of each card you'd like restored. For the most accurate quote, use good lighting so that any damaged areas are clear to see.

If you run into file size limits or any errors, please just upload 1 photo of all your cards together, and we will reach out to you.

You can select multiple images at once. Click Browse again to add more.

For each card, please list the card name and describe the restoration you'd like.

You can describe:

  • What looks damaged (creases, scratches, whitening, bends)
  • Where the damage is (corners, edges, front/back, holo area)
  • Any priorities (what matters most to you)

For example:

  • Charizard (Base Set, Holo) – slight surface scratching on holo area.
  • Pikachu (Jungle) – crease along left edge and light bend through center of card.
  • Gengar (Fossil) – corner wear on top right and small dent near border.

How can we reach you regarding your quote? Enter your phone number, email address, or Discord username.
